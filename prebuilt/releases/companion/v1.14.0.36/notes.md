## Companion firmware v1.14.0.36

Date: 2026-03-23

### Highlights
- Adds OTA update support to regular Heltec V4 and V3 companion builds (same OTA flow used on repeaters).
- Adds Meshcomod OTA commands on companion (`ota start`, `ota url <https://...bin>`, `ota status`).
- Keeps companion parity across all three meshcomod companion targets: V4 OLED, V4 TFT+touch, and V3.
